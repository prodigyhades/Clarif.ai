============= BUSINESS ANALYSIS REQUIREMENTS CLARIFICATION =============
# Problem Statement:
Business analysts frequently face ambiguous or incomplete requirements from stakeholders, leading to misaligned development efforts and project delays. Traditional clarification involves lengthy meetings and emails that slow progress. There is a need for an AI assistant that can interactively clarify ambiguous requirements, suggest missing details, and summarize stakeholder inputs to enhance requirement quality and alignment.

# Data Considerations:
Data includes historical requirements documents, stakeholder emails, and meeting transcripts in text formats. VOlume is limited to examples. Preprocessing involves text normalization and anonymization. Privacy considerations apply to sensitive project data. Synthetic requirement examples can be created for training and testing. Consider using synthetic or anonymized data where appropriate. Ensure data quality and relevance to the problem context.

# Expectation:
A prototype conversational GenAI agent that guides users through requirement clarification dialogue, identifies ambiguities and produces refined summaries. Features include natural language understanding, interactive questioning, and a simple chat UI. Success measured by user feedback on requirement quality and completeness. The demo will simulate requirement refinement sessions with sample inputs.
